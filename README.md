# LLM Cascading: The $500 Million Wake-Up Call

> Why intelligent routing is becoming the most important cost, governance, and sustainability decision in enterprise AI.

## Overview

As enterprise adoption of Generative AI accelerates, organisations are discovering a new challenge: uncontrolled inference costs.

Recent industry incidents have shown how quickly AI spending can spiral when every request is routed to the most expensive model by default.

This article explores:

- Why AI inference costs are becoming a board-level concern
- The rise of AI FinOps
- How LLM Cascading reduces cost while maintaining quality
- Research from Stanford's FrugalGPT and UC Berkeley's RouteLLM
- The environmental impact of AI inference
- Practical implementation guidance for enterprise teams

## Core Thesis

The future of enterprise AI is not about using the most powerful model.

It is about determining the smallest model capable of solving a problem with acceptable quality.

The smarter frontier models become, the more valuable intelligent routing becomes.
